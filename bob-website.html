Got Questions?

Here are the things people usually ask about:

What's the difference between Bob and other concierge services?
Bob combines AI with human expertise, lives right in your Telegram, has no monthly fees, and shows you actual progress in real-time. Most other services are either fully human (expensive), fully AI (limited), or require subscriptions.
How much does Bob cost?
Just a 12.5% service fee on what you actually spend. No subscription, no minimums, no hidden fees. If your task doesn't require a purchase, we'll quote you a fair price based on complexity.
How do I pay for stuff?
When a task requires payment, Bob sends you a secure payment link. You can pay with any major credit/debit card. Your payment info is never stored or shared with third parties beyond the payment processor.
Can Bob handle super specific or unusual requests?
Absolutely! The weirder or more specific your request, the more Bob shines. From tracking down discontinued sneakers to finding a barber who specializes in anime-inspired haircuts, we've got you.
How fast does Bob complete tasks?
It depends on the task. Simple restaurant bookings might take minutes, while tracking down rare items could take days. Bob always keeps you updated in real-time so you're never left wondering what's happening.
